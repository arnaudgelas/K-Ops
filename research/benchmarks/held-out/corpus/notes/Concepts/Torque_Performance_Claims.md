# Torque Performance Claims

## What It Is

The throughput claims made about Torque and how well they are corroborated.

## Why It Matters

A headline number repeated by many outlets can still rest on a single
vendor benchmark; independence matters for a decision.

## Key Claims

- The only first-party figure is 1.2M events/sec from the vendor benchmark ([[Sources/src-fac0000007|src-fac0000007]]).
- StreamWeekly ([[Sources/src-5ec0000012|src-5ec0000012]]) and DataEng Digest ([[Sources/src-5ec0000013|src-5ec0000013]]) both
  repeat 1.2M, but both derive from the same vendor benchmark -> NOT independent corroboration.
- A 5M events/sec claim ([[Sources/src-5ec0000016|src-5ec0000016]]) is far above the vendor figure and is
  retracted in the retraction snapshot; it supports no conclusion.
