# Source Summary: src-fac0000010

## Summary

GOVERNANCE.md defines how Torque is maintained.

## Key Claims

- All maintainer and merge rights are held exclusively by Torque Labs employees.
- External contributors may submit PRs but cannot approve or merge changes.
- Governance is therefore single-vendor controlled, not community-neutral.

## Evidence Notes

- Primary governance document. Directly contradicts the analyst memo
  (src-5ec0000015), which describes Torque as community-driven.
