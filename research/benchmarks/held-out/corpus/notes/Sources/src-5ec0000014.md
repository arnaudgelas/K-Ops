# Source Summary: src-5ec0000014

## Summary

A community discussion thread disputing Torque's exactly-once marketing.

## Key Claims

- Argues that Torque's so-called exactly-once is actually at-least-once
  delivery combined with downstream deduplication.
- Claims true exactly-once is not achievable across the Kafka sink in v2.1.0.

## Evidence Notes

- Community commentary; directly contradicts the v2.0.0 release-notes claim
  (src-fac0000001) that Torque provides exactly-once semantics.
