# Source Summary: src-fac0000003

## Summary

Issue #812 reports a memory leak in windowed aggregation under sustained high
load on Torque v2.1.0.

## Key Claims

- The leak reproduces on v2.1.0 with windows held open under sustained load.
- Heap grows unbounded until the worker is OOM-killed after roughly 40 minutes.
- Status in snapshot 01: OPEN / unresolved; a fix is planned for the next patch.

## Evidence Notes

- Maintainer-confirmed reproduction; primary bug tracker record.
